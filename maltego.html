Spearheading Africa's Structural Transformation.

Vision

A world free from the indignity of absolute poverty

Mission

To eradicate 100% of the planets absolute poverty by 2050

Our Story

We are a B Corp ,impact for-profit, startup tackling SDG 1 poverty.We envision a world free from poverty and are on a quest to establish sustainable social contracts on the Planet by leveraging technology-powered enterpreneurship by 2050.Our quest begins in Africa and subsequently the remaining undeveloped world.Africa is fast becoming the planets poverty epicenter.By 2030, the continent is projected to be home to 80% of the global poor.Solving at least 70% of the poverty crisis in Africa solves over 50% of the planet's poverty crisis. To successfully beat the poverty scourge, the fight must start in Africa.Our thesis for tackling SDG 1 creates a ripple effect in solving sdgs 2,4,6,8,9,10,11,12,14,15,16 and 17.

  • Phase 1 Agenda 2035

    • To achieve this,we have identified the path of least resistance in actualizing the market's structural transformation;movement of labour as a factor of production from low income activities to high income activities by the year 2035.By augmenting the value chains of 7 core verticles in the African Free market with technology,maximizing efficiencies thus unlocking full productivity of the sectors, we foresee Africa realizing her development as envisioned in the Agenda 2063 framework by 2035-2040; 28-23 years earlier

    • What is Agenda 2063

      • "We, the people of Africa and her Diaspora, united in diversity, young and old, men and women, girls and boys from all walks of life, deeply conscious of history, express our deep appreciation to all generations of Pan-Africanist. In particular, to the founders of the Organisation of African Unity for having bequeathed us an Africa with exemplary successes in the fight against slavery, colonialism and apartheid. Agenda 2063, rooted in Pan Africanism and African Renaissance, provides a robust framework for addressing past injustices and the realisation of the 21st Century as the African Century.

      We are confident that Africa has the capability to realise her full potential in development, culture and peace and to establish flourishing, inclusive and prosperous societies. We thus, commit to act together towards achieving the following aspirations:

      1. A prosperous Africa based on inclusive growth and sustainable development.

      2. An integrated continent,politically united and based in the ideals of Pan-Africanism and the vision of Africa's Renaissance.

      3. An Africa of good governance,democracy and respect for human rights,justice and the rule of law.

      4. A Peaceful and secure Africa.

      5. An Africa with a strong cultural identity,common heritage,shared values and ethics.

      6. An Africa whose development is people-driven,relying on the potential of the African people,especially its women and youth and caring for children.

      7. Africa as a strong, united and influential global player and partner.

        8. (African Union Commission, 2015)
